Rules

  • Participation in this prediction game is voluntary.
  • Each user is only allowed one single account.
  • Match Deadlines: All predictions must be submitted before the official kickoff. Late entries will be rejected by the system automatically.
  • Bonus Deadlines: Bonus parameters must be locked in before the official start of the WorldCup or will be also rejected.
  • Regular Time Only: Predictions apply exclusively to regular playing time (90m + stoppage). Extra time and penalties are ignored.
