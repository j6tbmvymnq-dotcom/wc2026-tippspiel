Protocol Rules

  • Participation in this prediction game is strictly voluntary.
  • Each operative is only allowed one single account.
  • Match Deadlines: All predictions must be submitted before the official kickoff. Late entries will be rejected by the system.
  • Bonus Deadlines: Bonus parameters must be locked in before the official start of the World Cup.
  • Regular Time Only: Predictions apply exclusively to regular playing time (90m + stoppage). Extra time and penalties are ignored.
