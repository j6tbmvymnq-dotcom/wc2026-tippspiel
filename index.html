General Rules

  • Participation in this prediction game is strictly voluntary.
  • Each player is only allowed one single account.
  • Match Deadlines: All match predictions must be submitted before the official kickoff time of the respective match. Late predictions will not be counted.
  • Bonus Deadlines: All bonus predictions must be submitted before the official start of the World Cup.
  • Regular Time Only: All predictions apply exclusively to the regular playing time (90 minutes + stoppage time). Extra time and penalty shootouts do not count!

Points System

Matches

3 pts = Exact Result (e.g., predicted 2:1, actual 2:1)

1 pt = Correct Outcome (e.g., predicted 2:0, actual 3:1)

Bonus Questions (BQ)

BQ 1 (10 pts): Correct World Cup Winner

BQ 2 (2 pts): Top 20 nation eliminated in the group stage

BQ 3 (2 pts): Nation achieving the highest victory (goal difference)

BQ 4 (2 pts): Group with the most exciting table (lowest point diff between top 3)

BQ 5 (5 pts): Number of matches ending with a 0:0 clean sheet

BQ 6 (5 pts): Number of group winners eliminated in the Round of 32
